Function Overload

A clash of creativity in which participant teams compete to create the best piece by completing an unfinished specimen of a classic genre.


Teams per school : 2
Members per team : 2

Prelims

The Preliminary round will take place on HackerRank with top 6 teams qualifying for the Finals.

Finals

The participants will be given a broad storyline which is to be coded into a Role-Player Game (RPG) game using Python/C++.
The participants will receive storylines on slack channel and then will be given a total time of 2 days to create their game with necessary mechanics. They will also be provided with a sample RPG game to get a broad idea.

Rules

• Participants can refer to the internet but directly copying exact codes will lead to the disqualification of the team.
• Any submissions after the deadline will not be considered.
• Partial codes can be submitted and points will be given accordingly.
• Submissions guidelines will be put up on the Slack Channel.



For more details, check the official Slack channel.

(In)vested Interests

Competitors with inventive minds and a spark for entrepreneurship will fight for the title in front of a team of experienced panelists, presenting the websites and pitching their ideas to prove that they are the finest innovators and second to none.


Teams per school : 1
Members per team : 4 (2 pitch 2 tech)


The participants will have to create a techbased MVP product or service that they will pitch to the judges. The product should aim at solving the online classes problem for the underprivileged sections of society.


The participants will be required to make a pitch deck for the same. It is advisable to include slides pertaining to the functionality, business model, USPs, market size, promotion and marketing strategies etc.It is beneficial to include multimedia content like 3D renders and mockups of the product, infotainment videos, animations etc.

Round-1

In the first round, participants will have to send us a document summarizing their idea and all resources they require for it, the pitch deck will be judged on the basis of content, originality, design etc.

Round-2

In the second round, a pitch will be conducted through video conferencing where the participants will be judged based on factors such as practical feasibility, economic viability, practicality, need etc.

More information will be provided on the Slack channel.



For more details, check the official Slack channel.

KiloHertz

Participants will have to make a track using the chord progression and intro melody as the fundamental basis of their track.


Teams per school : 1
Members per team : 1


• Contestants would be provided with Samples, Audio FXs and an unfinished track. The stems of the unfinished track would be also provided.
• They will have to make a track with the given chord progression and audio files as the fundamental musical structure for the track.
• Softwares Allowed : FL Studio
• Third Party Plugins Allowed : Generators: Sylenth1, Serum, Spire, reFx Nexus Effect: Fabfilter Bundle, Valhalla Bundle, iZotope Ozone, Ohmicide, LfoTool, CamelCrusher, OTT
• The track must be at least more than 75 sec long, with a drop.
• Participants must send the project file (.flp file) along with the samples and final exported audio (preferably .mp3 or .wav file).
• The finished audio piece would be judged on the basis of creativity and clarity of the mix.
• Submission Guidelines will be posted on the Slack Channel

More information will be provided on the Slack channel.



For more details, check the official Slack channel.
