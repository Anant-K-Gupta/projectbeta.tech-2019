Beta-test

Team event

Joining the holy trinity of events, we have this year, our very own cryptic hunt. Breaking the conventional 'input box' format, Beta-Test is a scavenger hunt incorporating answers and puzzles from throughout the internet and the rest is cryptic.

• Beta-Test starts on the 2nd October, 2019 continues for the next 48 hours. • Details can be found at www.projectbeta.tech • Any number of participants can participate from each team. • Points are awarded for every level cleared and bonus points are awarded to the teams placing 1st and 2nd.

(in)Vested Interests

Based on the popular competition, "TechCrunch...", this business pitching competition will give budding tech entrepreneurs the opportunity to pitch their ideas in front of 5 experienced panelists. They will also be given the chance to fight for the title of '(name of the title)'.

Prelims

• Participating schools are to send in a 1-2 page description of the product/service idea by 31st September '19. • 8 teams will be chosen for the on-stage round.

On-site round

• Participating schools are requested to present their ideas to an audience and the panel for 10 minutes. • This will be followed up by a 5 minute Q&A round by the panelists. • Relevant sub-headings are allowed & appreciated • The results will be based on the practicality and originality of the idea, the presentation, innovation and design.

func(64 Wit)

This is an amalgamation of two quintessential tech events, quiz and competitive programming. A team of 4 members (2 programmers + 2 quizzers) will be battling it out to attain the highest score.

On-site prelims

• The prelims for func(64 Wit) will be held on-site. • 8 teams will qualify for the stage round. • The rules for prelims will be announced on the spot.

On-stage round

• The event will be diversifying as the rounds progress. • Each team will be provided with 2 loaded machines. • Use of all languages is permissible. • The decision of the quiz masters will be final and binding. • The team with the higest aggregate wins, there will not be separate awards for quiz and programming. • In case of a tie, a rapid fire quiz round will be in order.

Rush_B

Every aspect of a true gamer. Sense of focus, leadership, game sense - are all put to test in our version of the classic CS:GO 5V5. Consisting of multiple rounds, Rush_B diversifies at every stage. Teams progress through the rounds and in the end, only one remains as the true winner.

Prelims

• Upon confirming the attendance, the participants will receive the details regarding the online prelims which will be taking place on 2nd October' 2019. • 8 teams of 5 each will be selected for the on-site event however, only one player can participate in the prelims from each team.

On-site round

• The on-site event will be a classic 8 team elimination bracket with teams of 5 each • The rules for judging will be shared through the Slack channel. • In case of a tie, a rematch would be in order. • Decision of the judges will be final and binding. • All participants are requested to bring their own hardware. Only basic peripherals except headphones would be provided.
